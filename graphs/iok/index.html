Welcome to the Index of Knowledge :)
This idea started off from course design from our Blockchain Fundamentals course, where we constructed dependency graphs for topics in our course material, avoiding cycles wherever they occurred for the sake of pedagogy. We realized that we could not only visualize topics taught in Blockchain Fundamentals, but the entire blockchain space as a whole: thus the idea of the Index of Knowledge was born.
